About Me

I'm Everett. My passion is web development, and it's been a large part of my life for years. I've built sites from the ground up and I've taken existing sites and breathed new life into them through creativity and persistence. Both can be exciting challenges, and there's nothing more rewarding than watching a project come together one step at a time, on the front and the back end.

I'm experienced in language, language, language and language. I love teaching myself new languages and new ways of doing things. I'm a perfectionist at heart, both with my own skillset and with the sites I work on. But you didn't come here to read, you came to see what I can do. Take a look at some of my past projects. We'll find out what we can do together.
